Technical Crash Investigation

September 16-27, 2019

NLETC

This course is eighty-hours (80) in length and emphasis will be on “hands-on” accident investigation. Students will do technical roadway and vehicle examination, and forensic examinations. The physics of vehicle dynamics and occupant kinematics will be studied. The students will do vehicle speed determinations to include: linear momentum and time distance calculations and applications. Outside testing projects will also be included.
